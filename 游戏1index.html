How to Play

1

Move your character around the board by swiping in the direction you want to go.

通过向您想要前进的方向滑动来移动您的角色。

2

Your goal is to fill the entire board with your color while avoiding obstacles.

您的目标是用您的颜色填满整个棋盘，同时避开障碍物。

3

Plan your moves carefully as you can't cross your own path or hit obstacles.

仔细规划您的移动，因为您不能穿过自己的路径或撞到障碍物。

4

Complete levels to unlock new challenges and color themes!

完成关卡以解锁新的挑战和颜色主题！

Color Fill 3D is an engaging puzzle game that challenges your spatial awareness and strategic thinking. As you progress through the levels, you'll encounter increasingly complex board layouts and obstacles that will test your problem-solving skills.
